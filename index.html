About the Area

Selva Val Gardena, located in the heart of the Dolomites, is a stunning mountain resort renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, world-class skiing, and charming alpine atmosphere. Nestled within a UNESCO World Heritage site, this picturesque village offers visitors a perfect blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation.

The area boasts over 500 kilometers of interconnected ski slopes as part of the Dolomiti Superski network, making it a paradise for winter sports enthusiasts. During the summer months, Val Gardena transforms into a haven for hikers, climbers, and mountain bikers, with numerous trails that showcase the region's dramatic peaks and lush valleys.

Whether you're looking to experience traditional South Tyrolean hospitality, indulge in delicious regional cuisine, or simply soak in the natural beauty, Val Gardena offers something for everyone. The charming villages of Selva, Ortisei, and Santa Cristina are filled with quaint shops, cozy cafes, and wellness centers to ensure a memorable stay.

Snowboarding in Val Gardena

Val Gardena is a top destination for snowboarding enthusiasts, offering a diverse range of slopes, snow parks, and off-piste opportunities that cater to all skill levels. The area is part of the Dolomiti Superski region, providing access to extensive trails with stunning views and excellent snow conditions throughout the season.

Snowboarders can enjoy thrilling descents on wide, well-groomed pistes, tackle challenging freeride routes, or practice tricks and jumps in dedicated snow parks such as the famous Piz Sella Snowpark. With modern lift systems and seamless connectivity to nearby resorts, exploring the vast terrain has never been easier.

For beginners, Val Gardena offers excellent ski schools with experienced instructors who provide lessons tailored to help riders build confidence and master their technique. Meanwhile, advanced snowboarders can take advantage of exciting slopes like the legendary Saslong World Cup downhill track for an adrenaline-packed experience.
